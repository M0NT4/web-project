About :

Our project is a CTF(Capture the flag) platform. players can create accounts and have access to many challenges ( web , pwn ,cryptography, forensics, steganography ...)

How To :

After successfully exploiting a challenge , a flag (a string having a remarquable format ) need to be submitted to get points . players can create a team or play solo ,they also can access the dashboard , check their ranks and submit their own writeups . the admin can add , delete and modify challenges . he have the right to send warnings or ban players in case of breaking rules .

Rules :

1. No DoS attacks of any kind

2. Don't share flags

3. Only one account per player is needed

4. Don't bruteforce flag submission
